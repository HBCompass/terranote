Dietrich Butte Fire

The Dietrich Butte Fire was ignited on Friday, June 27 by unknown causes. Approximately 3.5 miles north of Dietrich, Idaho, the fire consumed 3,195 acres of grass and brush. Forty personnel from Twin Falls Bureau of Land Management worked on containing the fire.
