Pavlof: Advisory Alert

Seismicity at the volcano has remained at low levels over the past 24 hours. Faint thermal features at the summit have been observed in satellite data and are consistent with cooling lava emplaced a few weeks ago. Pauses in vigorous eruptive activity of days to weeks are common during eruptive episodes of Pavlof Volcano, and a return to more robust eruptive activity remains possible and could occur with little or no warning.
